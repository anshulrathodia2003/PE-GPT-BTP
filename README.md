This repository is inspired by the research paper:  
> **PE-GPT: A Hybrid AI Framework for Power Electronics Design**  
> *(IEEE Transactions on Industrial Electronics)*  

The original work demonstrated how **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** and **Physics-Aware Neural Networks (PANN)** can work together to design power electronic converters (DAB, buck, etc.) and automate workflows like modulation strategy tuning, parameter selection, and simulation verification.

Our goal is to **open-source a similar framework** for the Power Electronics (PE) community, so that engineers, researchers, and students can easily experiment, extend, and apply this technology to their own design problems.
